Identity Of Woman Who Made Out With MJF During His Re-Bar Mitzvah Segment On AEW Dynamite

MJF celebrated his 27th birthday by having his Re-Bar Mitzvah in front of a jam-packed Winnipeg crowd on AEW Dynamite tonight. The AEW World Champion entered the Canada Life Centre with five woman by his side and made out with one of them. Now, it’s time to reveal the identity of the woman.

Click here for our full coverage of AEW Dynamite.

MJF kicked off the latest edition of Wednesday Night Dynamite with a huge celebration. The AEW World Champion was flanked by five woman as he walked to the ring for his Re-Bar Mitzvah.

MJF passionately kissed one of the woman before he entered the ring for the grand occasion. Obviously, this move was noticed by everyone watching.

A fitting entrance for #AEW World Champion @The_MJF; Happy Birthday to the Champ!

Watch #AEWDynamite LIVE on TBS!

For those wondering, the brunette who made out with MJF is Seleziya Sparx, an indie wrestler who has wrestled on AEW Dark in the past. Sparx took on Willow Nightingale on an episode of AEW Dark in October 2022. She has wrestled for various indie promotions including Pure Wrestling Association, Great Canadian Wrestling and Smash Wrestling.

MJF’s celebration was interrupted by ‘Jungle Boy’ Jack Perry, Sammy Guevara and Darby Allin. All three men said they wanted a shot at MJF’s title. The segment ended with the champ landed face-first on the cake that was set up on a ringside table.

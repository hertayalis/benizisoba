Who is Dotun Olubeko from The Bachelorette?

AFTER Zach Shallcross eliminated Charity Lawson from his season of The Bachelor, ABC granted her the chance to find love again.

The winner of The Bachelorette's 20th season ended up being Dotun Olubeko - Charity's number one choice.

Who is The Bachelorette's Dotun Olubeko?

Born on August 22, 1992, Dotun Olubeko is an integrative medicine specialist and reality TV contestant from Brooklyn, New York.

Known for appearing in The Bachelorette's 20th season, Dotun owns DPT Fitness; an online health and wellness service.

He's a graduate of both California State University, Chico, and the University of Western States; where he studied nutritional sciences.

Although he resides in Brooklyn, he and his family are originally from Nigeria.

"We came here with nothing, but my parents took on whatever job they could," he said about his parents and childhood during his series introduction.

"They've done really well to give me a life that I'm happy to live so it's been a really humbling experience."

Standing at six foot seven, he was also the tallest of Charity Lawson's competitors.

Dotun describes himself as "open-minded, full of love, and the best listener."

How far does Dotun get in The Bachelorette season 20?

Dotun ended the show as Charity Lawson's partner and the winner of season 20.

The second runner-up was fellow contestant Joey Graziadei.

How can I watch The Bachelorette?

When the show is on air, The Bachelorette hits ABC at 9pm ET every MOnday.

The Tuesday after the broadcast, the episodes are available via Hulu and Tubi TV.

On June 26, 2023, the program's 20th season premiered.

The reality series is a spinoff of ABC's original The Bachelor and made its network debut on January 8, 2003.

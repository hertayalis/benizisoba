Carl Fogarty Net Worth

Carl Fogarty, born on July 1, 1965 in Blackburn, England, is an English Superbike racer who is widely known as "Foggy". He has had an incredibly successful career in the sport, winning four World Superbike Championships. In 2014, he added another accomplishment to his list of achievements by winning the 14th series of I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here!

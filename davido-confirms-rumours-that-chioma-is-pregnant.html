Davido Confirms Rumours That Chioma is Pregnant

Davido and Chioma have been receiving all the attention and praises ever since news broke about their wedding in 2020. Davido let us in by posting a picture of the family after the introduction. It turns out there is another reason why the couple have decided to take the next step

Unlike before, the couple don’t flaunt eachother these days. We barely see multiple pictures of Chioma online. When celebrities are absent for months, it usually means one thing, a baby is on the way.

To add to our suspicion, Davido giggled and said “This one is special” when he was asked “Is Chioma Pregnant?” during his interview at Beat Fm. The singer seems very excited about Chioma’s pregnancy.

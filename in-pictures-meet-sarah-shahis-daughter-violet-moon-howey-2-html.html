In Pictures: Meet Sarah Shahis Daughter Violet Moon Howey

Spread the love

Violet Moon Howey, the daughter of former model and actress Sarah Shahi and actor Steve Howey, was born in the United States. The couple’s journey began when they got engaged on February 7, 2009, eventually tying the knot in Los Angeles and expanding their family to include three children.

The Twins – Violet and Knox

Known for their strong bond as a married couple, Sarah Shahi and Steve Howey welcomed their first child, a son named William Wolf Howey, in a unique home water birth. Subsequently, in January 2015, Shahi was expecting twins, and she gave birth to Knox Blue Howey and Violet Moon Howey in another at-home delivery, marking the arrival of their youngest children.

Violet Moon Howey’s Birth

Violet Moon Howey was born on March 1st, 2015, in Hollywood, California. At 7 years old, she embodies the traits of a Pisces, her astrological sign, as she grows up in the glitz and glamour of the entertainment industry.

The Love Story of Steve Howey and Sarah Shahi

The love story between Steve Howey and Sarah Shahi is a tale worth cherishing, having started with their engagement and culminating in a marriage that marked the beginning of their beautiful family.

Conclusion

Violet Moon Howey, born into a family of renowned personalities, navigates life’s adventures at a young age. Her story, intertwined with her parents’ love and separation, marks the beginning of her own journey, offering a glimpse into a life that balances the spotlight and the complexities of family dynamics.

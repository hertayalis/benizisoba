Beth Phoenix Shows Off With Pumped Up Gym Selfie

WWE Hall Of Famer Beth Phoenix was one of the pioneers of women’s wrestling in WWE during a time when female wrestlers were not given much of a focus. It seems Phoenix showed off the result of her efforts recently as well.

Beth Phoenix won multiple women’s titles during her time as an active competitor and her in-ring skills were second to none. Barring a few matches, Beth Phoenix was not active as an in-ring competitor in a long time.

That will be changing soon as she is now teaming up with her husband Edge and feuding with The Miz and Maryse in a feud that will culminate next week. Ahead of the match, Phoenix took to her Instagram and uploaded a gym selfie as she showed off her gains.

 I pick things up.

Edge and Beth Phoenix are set to square off against The Miz and Maryse in a mixed tag team match at the Royal Rumble pay-per-view. It remains to be seen how the match will turn out to be.

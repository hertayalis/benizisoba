Julius Randle Height: How Tall Is Julius Randle?

Julius Randle Height: How Tall Is Julius Randle?: Julius Randle, officially known as Julius Deion Randle is an American professional basketball player.

He developed a love for basketball at a younger age and has been consistent throughout his career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after basketball players.

At the time of this writing, Julius Randle played for the New York Knicks of the National Basketball Association.

He’s a two-time NBA All-Star and was awarded the NBA Most Improved Player Award in his second season with the Knicks in 2020–21.

Julius Randle kicked off his professional career in 2014 when he was selected as the seventh overall pick in the 2014 NBA draft by the Los Angeles Lakers.

In the 2016 off-season, he joined the US Select Team that practiced against the United States men’s national team ahead of the 2016 Summer Olympics.

On July 2, 2018, the Los Angeles Lakers renounced their rights to Randle, making him an unrestricted free agent, and on July 9, he signed a two-year, $18 million contract with the New Orleans Pelicans.

After a successful first year, Julius Randle declined his second-year player option on June 16, 2019, and became a free agent.

On July 9, 2019, he signed a three-year, $63 million contract with the New York Knicks. As of February 2023, he still played for the New York Knicks of the National Basketball Association.

Mac Miller net worth at the time of his death

Malcolm James McCormick, also called Mac Miller, was born on January 19, 1992, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Mac Miller was an American rapper, songwriter and record producer. Mac Miller started his career in Pittsburgh, and he used to do hip hop music as his genre of music.
Mac Miller is also credited with songs including the best day ever, self-care, come back to earth, and good news.
The late American rapper Mac Miller was signed on to the record label, Rostrum Records and also had several mixtapes like K.I.D.S and Best Day Ever.
Unfortunately, the young American rapper Mac Miller passed away on September 7, 2022, at the age of 26 years old. And according to several sources, he died through accidentally drug abuse.

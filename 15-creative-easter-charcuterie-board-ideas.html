15 Creative Easter Charcuterie Board Ideas

Easter is a holiday that provides many festive opportunities for decorations, food, and fun. If you’re looking for an idea that combines all three of those things—you’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’re sharing 15 Easter charcuterie board ideas that will make your family and friends hop with joy.

Need a venue to host your Easter brunch? Peerspace, the world’s largest online marketplace for short-term and hourly venue rentals has you covered. Start searching today to see the cornucopia of stellar venues available for your brunch, Easter egg hunt, or family gathering!

Not only are macaroons a delicious dessert worthy of any holiday, but they also tend to come in cute pastel colors. Making these sweet treats the focus of your charcuterie board will make it easy to coordinate the colors of the rest of your decorations and provide a wonderful photo—just be sure to take it before they’re all gobbled up!

Looking for Easter charcuterie board ideas that indulge your love of brie? This brie-cutout tutorial will show you how to carve a bunny into your block of brie! Not only is this pleasing to the eyes, it’s also pleasing to the taste buds!

We all know the Easter Bunny loves to chomp down on carrots, but the same can’t be said for every kid. If you’ve got a picky eater who would prefer not to see any carrots on their plate, instead opt for a crowd-pleasing carrot made of cheese.

Even though you’ll be making a charcuterie board, there’s still leeway to add other plates or dishes to enhance the theme. A simple bunny-shaped dish or platter can help to keep some of the sweeter offerings separate and provide a nice little splash of Easter decor.

If you’re looking for Easter charcuterie boards that will have you feeling like Spring is in the air, but still want to chow down on cured Italian meats, try turning your salami into little flowers. Everyone will be eager to rip the petals off of these salty and savory treats, so get them while they last!

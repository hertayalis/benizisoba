Catch that Shape Bandit is the name of the first game we have added into the Team Umizoomi Games category, which is one of the latest Nick Jr Games categories that we have created for our website, in which we are very thrilled to have added a new game for you all to play, knowing just how much you enjoy all the games based on series airing on this network! In Team Umizoomi, mathematical things like shapes play a really big part, and in this game, there is a bandit who has stolen the shapes in Umi-City, and you need to help our heroes get them back. How? Well, you will find right now, so keep reading the description, which will help you a lot, for sure! In order to catch the shape bandit, who has stolen the shape belt as well, you will have to help the team make things like a motorcyle or a boat, out of the different-shaped objects Bot provides. What you will do is very simple and easy: drag the shapes on the left side of the screen, onto the right one, over the blueprint, where that object fits the shape. When you put them all, your vehicle will be done. Simple as that! Good luck to you all, and we hope you have a great time!

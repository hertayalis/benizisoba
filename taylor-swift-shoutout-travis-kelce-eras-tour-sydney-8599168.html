Taylor Swift Gave Travis Kelce Romantic Shoutouts at Her Eras Show

Fans watching Taylor Swift’s Eras Tour performance last night in Sydney, Australia, were in for a special treat — and so was Travis Kelce.

While performing the song “Willow” from her 2020 album Evermore, a number of fans at the show believe she pointed right at Kelce (that's her boyfriend, if you’ve been living under a rock) while singing the lyric “That’s my man.”

Swifties were dissecting the move all over X, trying to confirm whether she did or did not, in fact, point at Kelce.

But there was a more definitive moment at last night’s show that clearly confirmed Kelce was on Swift's mind: She did her infamous lyric change during the song “Karma.” Instead of singing the lyric as written (“Karma is the guy on the screen, coming straight home to me"), she sang, “Karma is the guy on the Chiefs, coming straight home to me.” (She pulled this move once before, causing waves of fan freak-outs, at an Eras show in Argentina in November.)

Kelce flew to Sydney via private jet to be by his girlfriend’s side, and the two were photographed on an outing at the Sydney Zoo yesterday with friend and Swift's tour opener Sabrina Carpenter who was along for the ride.

It’s unclear how long the famous Kansas City tight end will be visiting Swift Down Under or how many shows he'll be attending — but it seems clear that Swift was happy to see him.

Younan Nowzaradan parents-Iranian-born American medical doctor, Younan Nowzaradan was born on October 11th, 1944 in Tehran in Iran.

His zodiac sign is Libra. His ethnicity is Persian, and he speaks Persian and English. Nowzaradan was raised in Iran, and in 1970, when he was 26 years old, he moved to the United States. At St. Louis University in Missouri, he completed the Medical Orientation Program in 1971.

In order to complete his rotating surgical internship residency, he then traveled to St. John’s Hospital in Detroit, Michigan. At St. Thomas Hospital in Nashville, Tennessee, he served a four-year surgical residency, and the Texas Heart Institute in Houston, Texas, provided him with a cardiovascular fellowship to round out his education.

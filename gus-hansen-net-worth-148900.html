Gus Hansen Net Worth

Gus Hansen is a Danish professional poker player born on February 13, 1974 in Copenhagen, Denmark. He has won several World Poker Tour titles and was named one of People magazine's 50 Sexiest Men in 2004. Hansen is known for his aggressive style of play and has become one of the most successful and recognizable poker players in the world.

Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott Share Romantic Beach Photo

Since they started dating in April 2017, Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott have managed to keep somewhat of a relatively low-profile (despite, you know, becoming new parents to baby Stormi). But today, the couple shared a rare and sweet PDA photo on Instagram.

In the picture posted by Kylie, she and her rapper boyfriend are embracing in front of a beachy sunrise.

Kylie, who appears to be makeup-free in the photo, wears a nude bandeau and army-inspired pants, while Travis is shirtless in a pair of orange bottoms. The new parents are both sporting braided hairstyles for the romantic couples photo op.

On February 1st, Kylie and Travis welcomed their first child, a baby girl named Stormi Webster. Jenner didn't confirm her pregnancy until after the birth of her daughter. The couple announced the news with an 11-minute documentary-style video titled 'To Our Daughter' on Youtube. Since the birth of her daughter, Kylie has taken to Instagram and Snapchat to share a few sweet selfies and videos with her newborn.

Over the weekend, Jenner sparked engagement rumors when she stepped out wearing a diamond band on her ring finger. Although the ring raised many questions, neither Kylie nor Travis has responded to rumors of an engagement. Their latest photo together, however, does kind of look like an engagement photoshoot...just saying.

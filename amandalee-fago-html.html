AmandaLee Fago - Age, Family, Bio

Popular social media star who is best recognized for the mukbang, recipe, and storytime videos she posted to her amandaleefago TikTok account. Her videos earned her over 2.8 million followers on the video platform. 

Before Fame

She launched her TikTok account in June 2017.

Trivia

Her first TikTok video to receive over 1 million views featured her giving her son juice in a bottle. She is from Staten Island, New York.

Family Life

Her husband Joey and son Dylan appeared on her TikTok account.

Associated With

She posted a TikTok duet with Britney Spears in May 2020.

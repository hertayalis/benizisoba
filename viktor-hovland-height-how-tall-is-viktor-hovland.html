How Tall Is Viktor Hovland?

Viktor Hovland is one of the most promising young golfers in the world today. With a slew of accomplishments to his name, it’s no wonder why the Norwegian athlete is attracting the attention of fans and experts alike.

Apart from his skills on the course, many people are also curious about his physical build, particularly his height.

In this article, we’ll take a closer look at Viktor Hovland’s height and explore why it matters in the world of golf.

Viktor Hovland’s Height: How Tall is He?

So, just how tall is Viktor Hovland? According to various sources, the golfer stands at around 6 feet tall (or 183 centimeters).

This puts him at a slightly above-average height for a male golfer, as the average height for a PGA Tour player is around 5’10”.

Viktor Hovland weighs roughly 165 lb (75 kg).

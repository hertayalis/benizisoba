Is Tom Sturridge Still Together With Girlfriend Maya Hawke?

Tom Sturridge seems to be still together with his girlfriend, Maya Hawke.

Although they were hanging out with each other, whether they were romantically involved in a relationship with each other or not was vague. But things seem to be clear when they were photographed being cozy and kissing each other in a local restaurant in August 2020.

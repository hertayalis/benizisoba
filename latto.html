Who is Latto?

Latto birthed as Alyssa Michelle Stephens, was born on December 22, 1998, in Columbus, Ohio to Shayne and Misti. She relocated to Clayton County, Georgia, with her parents when she was two years old. Stephens began writing her own rap music when she was ten years old, and she also competed in drag racing. She attended Lovejoy High School as a teen, where she was constantly ridiculed by her peers owing to her skin color, which was a mix of her black father and white mother. In order to embrace the racial designation of “mulatto,” Stephens used the stage moniker Miss Mulatto when she began her rapping career. This was eventually abbreviated as Latto.

Chrissy Teigen &amp; John Legend Expecting Their Third Child Together

  • Chrissy Teigen and John Legend are reportedly expecting their third child together and revealed the news in Legend's music video for his new song, "Wild."
  • Toward the end of the video, Teigen looks adoringly down at her stomach as she cradles her belly.
  • Teigen and Legend have been married since 2013 and have two children, Luna and Miles Stephens, together.

Update, 8/14:

Chrissy Teigen has confirmed her third pregnancy after she and husband John Legend teased the news in a new music video. Taking to Twitter, the model and TV personality shared a video of herself posing in the mirror with a black blazer and leggings, holding her baby bump.

"Look at this third baby shit," she muses in the clip. "What the ... Oh my God."

Original story, 8/13:

Chrissy Teigen and John Legend are about to become a family of five!

The longtime couple shared the news of their growing family in Legend's music video for his new song, "Wild." In the dreamy visuals starring the couple enjoying a sultry and romantic outing on a beach, Teigen seemingly teases the baby news. While wearing a white linen crop top and matching maxi skirt, the camera zooms in on Teigen cradling what fans speculate is a baby bump.

Multiple sources also confirmed the pregnancy to People. Teigen and Legend have yet to officially confirm the news, but following the video's premiere, Teigen did take to her favorite social media platform, Twitter, to share a smiling, loved-up emoji, hinting at her excitement over the news. She also retweeted a Vevo tweet saying the music video ends with a "beautiful surprise."

This would be the third child for Teigen and Legend, who already share a daughter and son together: Luna Simone Stephens, 4, and Miles Theodore Stephens, 2.

Teigen is no stranger to making an appearance in her husband's music videos. The couple first met, per People, in 2006 on the set of his music video for "Stereo." Coming full circle, the video for "Wild" is directed by filmmaker Nabil Elderkin, who introduced the two all those years ago.

Maddie Pacheco of Bristol dies unexpectedly

Bristol, RI – The community of Bristol, Rhode Island, mourns the passing of Madisyn “Maddie” Pacheco, a cherished 2019 graduate of Mt. Hope High School, known for her devotion to soccer and vibrant spirit. Maddie’s presence on the field was described as pure magic, earning admiration for her athleticism and unwavering commitment as a center-back player.

Her teammates, faculty, and coaches remember Maddie as a fierce competitor and a joy to play alongside, both on and off the field. The Women’s Softball Team at Jack’s Bar/Market Street Pub highlighted her natural athletic prowess, recalling her swift integration into their tournament team during the “Corey Won’t Fight Alone” games.

Cornhole for a Cause posted on Facebook,

“It is with great sadness to announce the loss of a beloved member in the Cornhole for a Cause family. On Saturday, November 18th we lost a one of a kind, beautiful young lady, Maddie Pacheco. Maddie has played in Cornhole for a Cause since the very first year and never failed to put a smile on everyone’s face. To the entire Pacheco family, Cornhole for a Cause is sending you love and prayers during this time and are here for you near and far!
We love you all so very much.
Hannah Nasser”

Throughout her time at Mt. Hope High School and beyond, Maddie Pacheco left an indelible mark as a positive force, radiating light in the lives of those around her. Her teachers, coaches, and peers echo sentiments of Maddie’s unwavering positivity and kindness, traits she embodied daily.

In the wake of this tragic loss, the community extends its thoughts and prayers to the entire Pacheco family, urging solidarity during their period of unimaginable grief. Details regarding Maddie Pacheco’s obituary and funeral arrangements will be shared by her family, offering an opportunity for friends, teammates, and well-wishers to honor and bid farewell to a remarkable soul whose light shone brightly among them.

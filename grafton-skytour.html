Grafton Sky Tour | Enjoy Illinois

Rise to new heights with Grafton Sky Tour's aerial lift.

Enjoy the best view in the Midwest!  Take an enclosed gondola or open chair lift and enjoy a soaring, 300 foot high, scenic, leisurely ride up the bluff top to Aerie's Resort, Winery, and other amenities. The Sky Tour, overlooking the confluence of the Mississippi and Illinois rivers is a breathtaking and relaxing way to enjoy the spectacular beauty of the region.

Tickets are $10 per person for a round trip pass and Season tickets are available for $29 per person. Please call 618-786-8439 for information.

Ticket Hours Of Operation

Mon-Thu: 10:45am-7:00pm

Fri-Sat: 9:45am-9:00pm

Sun: 9:45am-7:00pm
